## How to Use These Templates

1. **Select 3-5 goals** that align with your role and interests
2. **Customize** the specifics (dates, metrics, scope) to your situation
3. **Add 1-2 personal goals** not covered here
4. **Discuss with your team lead** (Remco or Bram) to validate alignment
5. **Document in Workday** by end of January 2026
6. **Schedule regular SYNC-ups** to track progress

## Goal Quality Checklist (SMART)

Before finalizing, ensure each goal is:
- [ ] **Specific** - Clear what you're achieving
- [ ] **Measurable** - Has concrete success metrics
- [ ] **Achievable** - Stretching but realistic
- [ ] **Relevant** - Supports team/company objectives
- [ ] **Time-bound** - Has a target date
