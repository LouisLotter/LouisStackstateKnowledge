# SUSE Technology & Product Organizational Goals - FY2026

*Organization: Technology & Product (Thomas Di Giacomo)*

---

## Goal 1: AI Productivity & Adoption

**Goal:** Achieve 20% productivity gain and 30% AI adoption by end of Q2, scaling to 40% productivity gain and 80% adoption by end of Q4.

**Description:** Achieve measurable productivity increase in key T&P efficiency metrics per function (e.g., development cycle time, time to resolution, or documentation creation time) attributable to new AI tools/initiatives.

---

## Goal 2: Rancher Community Conversion

**Goal:** Convert 2% (200 new customers) of the SAM (~9.7K ICP from 55K active users) Rancher Community customers to Rancher Prime/Suite.

**Description:** The primary activities with Operation Goldmine are:
- De-anonymize Rancher Community enterprises that match SUSE's ICP
- Work with each geo on outreach and measurement programs

*Note: Corporate marketing are also running targeted campaigns against the named customers*

---

## Goal 3: R&D Cost Efficiency

**Goal:** Decrease R&D cost of revenue to 18% by FY27 through AI and process optimization.

**Description:** Cost % of Revenue - R&D reduction through the use of AI and process optimization.

---

## Goal 4: Gross Retention (ECM)

**Goal:** Increase Gross Retention across all products to 88% by end of FY27.

**Description:** ECM Gross Retention improvement across the product portfolio.

---

## Goal 5: Edge Strategy Maturity

**Goal:** Mature a stable Near Edge offering supporting majority of industry use cases and close 5 new customers by end of FY26.

**Description:** The goal is to mature a stable Near Edge offering that supports the majority of industry use cases, regardless of scale.

---

## Goal 6: Employee NPS (eNPS)

**Goal:** Achieve an eNPS score that meets or exceeds the company score.

**Description:** For any team with a score below the benchmark, implement a documented people plan.

---

## Goal 7: Portfolio Strategy Execution

**Goal:** Deliver quality solutions in support of corporate ACV and Revenue growth targets.

**Description:** Support CRO to achieve Revenue and ACV growth targets across the portfolio (SMLS, AI, Edge, Emerging, SAP, SLES).

---

## Goal 8: Net Retention

**Goal:** Increase Net Retention across all products to 110% by end of FY27.

**Description:** Group Net Retention improvement across the product portfolio.

---

## Goal 9: SUSE AI Product-Market Fit

**Goal:** Grow and mature the SUSE AI offering, establishing product-market-fit in support of $12.97M ACV bookings.

**Description:** Plug AI-specific capability gaps to mature the offering, become more competitive, and create an easy-to-use offering focused on enterprises with a high need for protecting their IP (data and business processes).

---

## Goal 10: Customer Value & Uptime

**Goal:** Improve perceived customer value (+5% NPS, >99.9% uptime) across the product portfolio.

**Description:** Drive best-in-class product management practices, better insights integration, smoother customer experience (software delivery, Cloud transactions, subscription management), deeper integration with AWS, and expanded portfolio across CSPs.

---

## Goal 11: Unified Core (SKE) Adoption

**Goal:** Have >3 distinct SUSE products/solutions utilizing UC/SKE as the base runtime by end of FY26, and SKE launched as a distinct Kubernetes runtime/distribution.

**Description:** Target products include Rancher Prime, SUSE AI, SUSE Edge, SUSE Telco Cloud.
