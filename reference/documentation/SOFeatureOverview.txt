Improved Collaboration and Decision-Making: Foster shared understanding and enable data-driven actions based on cross-domain insights.
Developer Empowerment: Equip developers with the tools they need for self-service diagnostics and troubleshooting in production ("build it, run it").
Enhanced Operational Efficiency: Streamline workflows, reduce tool sprawl, and simplify operations, especially when integrated with SUSE Rancher Prime.
SUSE Cloud Observability empowers organizations to navigate complexity with confidence, ensuring application reliability, optimizing performance, and ultimately accelerating innovation by providing unparalleled clarity, context, and control over their entire IT estate.

Unique Differentiators: See Your Systems Like Never Before
These core capabilities provide unparalleled context and insight, setting SUSE Observability apart:


Unified Time-Traveling Topology & Telemetry


Description: SUSE Observability uniquely combines real-time Topology (your system's components and their dynamic relationships) with Telemetry (metrics, events, logs) and Tracing (request flows), all versioned over Time. Instead of viewing data in silos, our platform automatically correlates these dimensions, providing immediate, rich context for every observation. This contextual engine understands how your system is structured, how it performs, and how it evolves moment by moment.  
Benefits:
Eliminate Blind Spots: Gain a holistic, contextual understanding of your entire IT landscape, breaking down data silos that hinder troubleshooting.  
Unlock Deeper Insights: Understand the intricate relationships between application performance, infrastructure changes, and user experience.
Foundation for Faster Resolution: Provides the essential context needed for rapid root cause analysis and proactive prevention.  

Real-Time Topology Visualization & Dependency Mapping


Description: Automatically discover and visualize the complete, real-time map of your applications, microservices, containers, infrastructure, and their dynamic dependencies. This isn't a static diagram ‚Äì it's a living blueprint of your environment, continuously updated as components are added, removed, or changed.  
Benefits:
Instant Clarity: Immediately understand complex system structures and how components interact without manual mapping.  
Visualize Impact: See the potential blast radius of issues or changes instantly, understanding upstream and downstream effects.  
Navigate Complexity: Intuitively explore intricate architectures, making distributed systems understandable.  
Improve Collaboration: Provide a shared, accurate view for DevOps, SRE, and platform teams, speeding up incident response.  

Guided Remediation & Intelligent Notifications
Description: Move faster from detection to resolution with Guided Remediation. SUSE Observability automatically contextualizes related health violations, directly linking the cause of an issue to its effects across your applications and infrastructure. Leverage curated, step-by-step troubleshooting guides, especially for complex Kubernetes environments, to empower teams to resolve issues efficiently. Integrate seamlessly with your existing workflows via webhooks and deep integrations with tools like Slack and Microsoft Teams, providing contextual, real-time updates to the right people in the right channels.  
Benefits:
Accelerate Resolution: Significantly reduce Mean Time To Resolution (MTTR) with automated context and guided troubleshooting steps.  
Empower Teams: Enable developers and SREs to resolve issues faster with proven, curated remediation guidance.  
Streamline Incident Response: Deliver timely, contextual notifications directly into collaboration tools, improving communication and reducing noise.  
Reduce Operational Burden: Minimize the time spent manually correlating alerts and searching for resolution steps.

Time-Travel Analysis


Description: Rewind the state of your entire environment ‚Äì topology, health, configuration, events, and telemetry ‚Äì to any specific moment in the past. Investigate incidents precisely as they happened, analyze the impact of changes, and debug transient issues without needing to reproduce complex failure scenarios.  
Benefits:
Solve Elusive Bugs: Diagnose intermittent or complex issues that are impossible to reproduce reliably.  
Accelerate Root Cause Analysis: "Rewind the movie" to see the exact sequence of events and changes leading to an incident, drastically reducing investigation time.  
Understand Change Impact: Precisely compare system state before and after deployments or configuration changes.  
Eliminate Reproduction Hassle: Save valuable engineering time by analyzing historical data instead of trying to recreate failures.  

eBPF-Powered Monitoring


Description: Leverage the power of eBPF (Extended Berkeley Packet Filter) for highly efficient, low-overhead monitoring directly within the Linux kernel. Capture granular network communication data (including Rate, Errors, Duration - RED signals for common protocols like HTTP, gRPC and SQL), discover service dependencies, and gain deep visibility without modifying application code or impacting performance.  
Benefits:
Deep Visibility, Minimal Impact: Get detailed network and process insights with negligible performance overhead, ideal for resource-sensitive container environments.  
Automatic Discovery: Effortlessly map service communications and dependencies in real-time without manual configuration.  
Code-Free Insights: Obtain critical application communication metrics without requiring code instrumentation, simplifying deployment.  

Dynamic Thresholding & AI-Driven Insights
Description: Go beyond static alerts with intelligent Dynamic Thresholding. This feature autonomously learns the normal behavior of your metrics, understanding seasonality and patterns with zero configuration. It operates with low overhead and scales effectively, automatically identifying significant deviations that provide early warnings for potential issues. Apply it easily to business KPIs and critical service metrics to catch problems before they impact users.   
Benefits:
Proactive Problem Prevention: Detect subtle issues based on deviations from learned normal behavior before they impact users or trigger static alerts.
Reduce Alert Noise: Focus on statistically significant anomalies that matter, minimizing false positives and alert fatigue.   
Effortless Intelligence: Gain immediate value from AI-powered insights without complex setup or manual threshold tuning.
Faster Root Cause Analysis: Combine anomaly detection with topology and change data (Time-Travel) to quickly narrow down potential causes.
Automatic Resource Dashboards with Time-Travel


Description: Gain immediate insights with automatically generated dashboards for every discovered resource (service, pod, node, VM, etc.) in your environment. These dynamic dashboards consolidate critical data, including highlights, key metrics, events, logs, traces, related resources, and health status over time, onto a single screen. Fully integrated with Time-Travel, you can instantly view the complete state of any resource dashboard ‚Äì including configuration and all associated signals ‚Äì as it existed at any point in the past.  
Benefits:
Instant Resource Visibility: Get a comprehensive overview of any resource's health and performance without manual dashboard configuration.  
Eliminate Context Switching: Access all relevant MELT data, events, health timelines, and related components for a specific resource in one place, speeding up troubleshooting.  
Historical Contextual Debugging: Use Time-Travel to see exactly how a resource's dashboard looked (including metrics, logs, config, health) at the precise moment an issue occurred.  

Extensible Integration Framework (StackPacks)
Description: SUSE Observability is easily extensible. You can create monitors, metric/trace/topology bindings, remediation guides, and dashboards, packaging them into StackPacks for easy sharing within the community. Leveraging OpenTelemetry standardized data input and semantic conventions simplifies integrating almost any technology. SUSE provides StackPacks for SUSE technologies and deep, out-of-the-box integrations for Kubernetes distributions including K3s, RKE2, EKS, AKS, GKE, and OpenShift. Integrating your own data via OpenTelemetry and mapping it to SUSE Observability concepts is straightforward, promoting knowledge reuse.  
Benefits:
Rapid Integration: Quickly connect and monitor diverse technologies using pre-built or custom StackPacks.  
Community & Reusability: Share and leverage integrations built by SUSE and the wider community.
Standardized Approach: Easily integrate custom or new technologies using OpenTelemetry standards.  
Deep SUSE & K8s Support: Benefit from out-of-the-box, deep integrations for core SUSE products and major Kubernetes distributions.

Comprehensive Observability: All Your Signals, Unified and Contextualized
SUSE Observability provides robust support for the essential observability signals, enhancing each with the power of its contextual engine:
Metrics Collection & Analysis


Description: Ingest, store (using VictoriaMetrics ), and analyze time-series metrics from applications, infrastructure, Kubernetes, cloud services, and external tools. Visualize metrics on dashboards, define alerts, and explore data, all automatically correlated with the system topology and timeline. 
Benefits:
Track KPIs: Monitor key performance indicators and resource utilization.  
Identify Trends: Understand normal behavior and spot performance deviations.  
Contextual Alerting: Receive alerts that inherently understand the affected component and its potential impact.  
Event Monitoring & Correlation


Description: Capture and correlate significant events like deployments, configuration changes, scaling activities, health state changes, external alerts, and detected anomalies. Events are timestamped and automatically linked to the relevant topology components and timeline.  
Benefits:
Track Changes: Understand the impact of critical system activities.  
Correlate Events to Issues: Easily link performance changes or failures to specific preceding events.  
Maintain Audit Trail: Keep a historical record of significant system changes and activities.  
Log Aggregation & Analytics


Description: Collect, centralize (using Elasticsearch ), and analyze log data from across your environment. Access logs directly within the context of the specific component (pod, service, VM) in the topology view.  
Benefits:
Deep Dive Troubleshooting: Analyze detailed log messages to understand specific errors and operational details.  
Contextual Log Access: Quickly navigate from a problematic component to its relevant logs, avoiding noise.  
Distributed Tracing


Description: Capture and visualize end-to-end request flows through distributed systems using OpenTelemetry standards (OTLP). Store traces (using ClickHouse ) and correlate them with topology and telemetry for richer analysis.  
Benefits:
Pinpoint Bottlenecks: Identify latency sources within complex transactions.  
Understand Service Interactions: Visualize request paths across microservices.  
Debug Distributed Issues: Troubleshoot errors spanning multiple services effectively.  

Enterprise Dashboarding
Enterprise Dashboarding provides enterprise-grade visualization capabilities, offering robust query editors, diverse panel support, and interactive exploration. Uniquely designed to drive Cost Optimization and enhanced operational efficiency-, enable Data-Driven Business Overviews and collaborative team reporting, and accelerate Root Cause Analysis. Our platform deeply integrates dashboard creation into the operational and investigative lifecycle: Widgets from any dynamic resource dashboard can be instantly promoted to new or existing permanent dashboards, accelerating proactive monitoring; Investigation findings captured in Pinned Items can be formalized and shared with a single click, transforming diagnostic narratives into reusable knowledge assets for collaboration; and most critically, dashboard widget retains its source link, enabling immediate contextual drill-down back to the originating resource-specific automatic resource dashboards if applicable, ensuring users to keep context and drastically reduce the Mean Time To Context (MTTC) during incident response.
